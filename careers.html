Compliance Specialist

Job Position:

Compliance Specialist

(Industrial Ammonia Refrigeration)

Compensation:

$75,000 - $90,000 Annually

Responsibilities:

• Oversee multiple customers’ compliance programs simultaneously.

• Facilitate end to end Process Hazard Analysis.

• Complete accurate Compliance Audits and GAP Analysis’.

• Perform close out meetings for Mechanical Integrity Audits including recommendation summary.

• Author, develop and maintain compliance program materials.

• Complete accurate Management of Change requests for customers.

• Assist engineering team with projects.

• Travel to and from customer locations.

• Occasional overnight travel to customers.

• Maintain expert knowledge of industry codes and standards.

Company Overview:

New England Ammonia Safety Inc, (NEAS) is a rapidly growing new company in the Northeast Region, USA who specializes in compliance, design, and service for the ammonia refrigeration industry. NEAS aims to make a positive impact on the industry by adhering to the highest standards for the success of its customers. The founders of this company bring together over 50+ years of experience making them the experts of Ammonia Refrigeration, EHS and Compliance Program Development.

New England Ammonia Safety’s Company culture includes positive and highly motivated professionals who work both well with others and independently to get the job done. With the flexibility of working remotely, onsite and in the office means that NEAS provides a way to communicate, collaborate and work together even when our team members are in different places. Our flexible working environments means we can adapt to our customers’ needs, our employees’ needs and have the tools to pivot for growth. The working environment varies on roles and responsibilities as well as customer locations.
