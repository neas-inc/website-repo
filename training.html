Operator 1 Training

New England Ammonia Safety offers customized training solutions for your facility and staff. OSHA requires all PSM/RMP facilities to facilitate Operator Training that complies with OSHA’s 29 CFR 1910.119 (g)”Training” section. NEAS has the knowledge and, more importantly, the experience to effectively train your staff to help operate your facility safely.

Duration: 3 Days / 24 Hours

Course Material:

• OSHA / EPA / PSM/RMP / GDC Overview

• Ammonia Safety

• RAGAGEP

• Basic & Complex Refrigeration Cycles

• Thermodynamics

• Equipment

• Fundamental Tasks

Ammonia Awareness Training

If your facility operates an Ammonia Refrigeration system, then you are required to administer Ammonia Awareness Training to all of your staff and visitors annually. New England Ammonia Safety makes site specific Ammonia Awareness Training Modules for you and your staff. Knowing important information about ammonia properties, first aid requirements, and muster points in case of an emergency can make all the difference.

NEAS can accommodate any facility or staff size, language interpretation, and multiple shifts.
